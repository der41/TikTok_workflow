# Privacy Policy for Astralosophy Content Automator

Last Updated: March 3, 2026

### 1. Information We Collect
Our application, Astralosophy Content Automator, only collects the minimum data required to upload videos to TikTok. This includes:
- Account Information: Your TikTok username and profile picture (via the Login Kit) to verify the destination account.
- Video Content: The video files you manually select from your device to upload.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the requested permissions (specifically the video.publish scope) solely to transmit your selected video and its associated metadata (caption, hashtags) to your TikTok profile.

### 3. Data Storage and Retention
- Local Processing: Video files are processed locally on your machine. We do not store your videos on any external servers.
- No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Data is only sent to TikTok’s official API to fulfill the upload request.

### 4. User Control
You can revoke this app’s access at any time through your TikTok account settings under "Security and Login" > "Manage App Access."
